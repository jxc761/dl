Quantitative evaluations
Measurement in origin depth space
  • Average relative error: $$\frac{1}{P} \sum_p \frac{| x_p-\tilde{x_p} | }{x_p}$$
  • Root mean squared error: $$\sqrt{\frac{1}{P} \sum_p(x_p-\tilde{x}_p)^2}$$
  • Percentage of bad matching pixels: $$ \frac{1}{P}\sum_p ( |x_p-\tilde{x}_p | > \delta )$$
  • Accuracy with threshold($\delta$): $$ \frac{1}{P}\sum_p \left( \max\left( \frac{x_p}{\tilde{x_p}} ,\frac{\tilde{x_p}}{x_p} \right)< \delta \right)$$
